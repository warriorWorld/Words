According to the letter, the university will allow its undergraduate students to take graduate classes and get scores from them. This is because they think this can attract undergraduate students to apply to their own school as a graduate school.
The man in the conversation disagrees with this idea.
First, he believes that seminars should be small and only in this way can students have a good discussion. And also, he says that those undergraduate students don't have enough background knowledge, as a result, they will slow down graduate students' progress.
Second, he says that selecting a different graduate school can widen your horizons.
