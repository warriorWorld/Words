The reading is about chain behavior, it states that parents can teach their children step by step and chain the steps together, that is why it is called chain behavior.
The professor elaborates on this by providing an example. He mentions he taught his daughter how to wash her hands. He separated washing hands into five steps, which are:
1, turn on the water.
2, wet your hands.
3, get soap on your hands.
4, wash your hands.
5, turn off the water.
He taught his daughter one step by one step, and each step his daughter practiced each for a few days, and then chain them together.
