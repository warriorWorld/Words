The reading is about advertising with images, it states that the advertising image is usually positive and entertaining. And you need to ink this impression into your customers' minds.
The professor elaborates on this by providing an example. Which is the big bear cookies. The big bear cookies show their images on TV shows, and the cookies itself is bear-shaped. And children really love it, because it often dances and says funny things in its TV shows. And people often relate bears with cookies, even if they are not related. In addition, the professor mentioned that the company sells more cookies than other companies.
