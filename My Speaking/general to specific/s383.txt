The reading is about phoresy, it states that phoresy is a kind of temporary relationship that when an animal uses other animals to travel around with no harm or a little harm done to the host animal.
The professor elaborates on this by giving an example.
Mites is a kind of tiny animal that eats flower pollen. It is difficult for a mite to climb down a flower and then climb up another flower. And mites use humming birds to travel from one flower to another due to the humming birds also eating the same kind of flowers. In addition, this will not hurt the humming bird.
