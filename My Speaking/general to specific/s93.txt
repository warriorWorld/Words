The reading is about establishing shots. It states that establishing shots can provide audiences with background information and also a mood behind the scene.
The professor mentioned himself as an example of this. He once watched a movie. At first, the movie showed to the professor a big city, a lot of skyscrapers in the city, and the building fashion was old, maybe from the 1940s. The light in the scenery was very dark and gloomy. This shot gives the professor the information that this story happened in the 1940s in a big city and the story is dark and mysterious.
