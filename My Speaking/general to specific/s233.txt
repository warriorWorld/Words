The reading is about reference groups. It states that most people would follow others behaviors and thinking, and through their life, they would change their reference groups accordingly.
The professor mentioned when he first went to a university, he met a lot of students that he considered cool, and they always wear some casual clothes, so the professor imitated them and also wore jeans, T-shirts, and sneakers.
And when the professor went to work in Chicago, he met a lot of young men who already take some major responsibilities for themselves. And the professor admired them a lot. So he imitated them and also wore very formal clothes.
