The reading is about comfort zone bias. It states that when people make choices they tend to select the one that they are familiar with. 
The professor mentioned his friend as an example of this.
The professor's friend is a film major. And he loves films, he wants to be a film reviewer because in this way, he can watch films without paying. But eventually he only found a news reporter job. And overtime, he was already familiar with the reporter job and had confidence with it. And one day, the film reviewer quit his job, then, the professor's friend had an opportunity to take that film reviewer job. But he refused that opportunity even though the job paid more.
