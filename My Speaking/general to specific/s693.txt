The reading is about brand revival. It states that customers may want some old brands to revive, and some companies may revive old products and add some new features to them.
The lecturer mentioned cars as an example of this. He mentioned a certain small car was really cute and popular in the past, and ten years ago, the company decided to revive the car. Because they thought the car would attract a lot of people since the car was very convenient in cities. And they changed the car's engine in order to make it more gas-efficient since the original car was very gas-consuming. Nowadays, the car is very popular.
