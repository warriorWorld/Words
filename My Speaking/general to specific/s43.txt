The reading is about verbal and nonverbal communication. It states that when those two kinds of communication according to each other, the communication can be amplified, by contrast, if they are not, the communication can lead to messages that we don't intend to.
The professor mentioned two examples in order to elaborate on this.
First, he mentioned his uncle Peter visited him, and he was surprised, he opened his eyes wide and had a big smile. And his uncle was really happy because he was surprised about the professor's reaction.
Second, he mentioned he once accidentally hurt himself by a hammer in front of his daughter, and he said he is ok to his daughter, but his daughter didn't believe him because he expressed pain through his tremble and his face.
