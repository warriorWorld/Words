The reading is about masking, it states that since one sound may cover the other, too much noise has already affected a lot of animals' survival, because animals need to use sound to communicate or do other things.
The professor mentioned the largest species of whale as an example of this. This kind of whale can produce a special sound that can help them to locate their prey, detect the shape of their surroundings and lead to their next meal. Researchers have found on the west coast of Canada that the increased boat noise has reduced the size of this kind of whale hunting area by 20 percent.
