The reading is about entertainment merchandising. It states that some products can use entertainment shows in order to advertise themselves.
The professor mentioned himself as an example of this. When he was young, he really loved the anime called Action Hero, and he often pretended to be the hero. One day he saw a toy in a store which is from the Action Hero.
And then the professor mentioned another example of this which is his seven year old daughter who really loves a cute little doll. And one day his daughter told him that the anime in which its protagonist is the little doll Rossa would start to play on television. And soon, the program became popular very quickly.
