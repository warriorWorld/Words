The reading is about target marketing, it states that nowadays, commercials like to target their market on specific groups of people.
For example, a cellphone company may make an advertisement for young people on a TV show. In this advertisement, they show the phone's bright colour, and show you that you have a good time with your friends.
And still the cellphone company, but this time, they want to sell the phone to businessmen. They show the phone can be easily handled and can even help you to save money. Even those are the same phone.
