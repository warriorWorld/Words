The reading is about flagship species, it states that some experts would select some species as flagship species in order to raise people's awareness of environmental protection.
The professor mentioned macaws as an example. Macaw is a kind of colorful bird who lives in rain forests. And there is a rainforest, a lot of trees in that forest have cutted down by local people. Some experts selected the macaw as flagship species, and they printed a lot of books that contained the macaw pictures in them, and sent the books to schools and communities in that area. Finally, people there were affected by this effort, and the forest was recovered, the macaw population along with a lot of other species population such as other birds, bats, and frog population also recovered.
