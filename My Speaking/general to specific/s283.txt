The reading is about Refute-And-persuade, it states that some companies would first accept some disadvantages of their products, and then persuade customers that the disadvantages actually are advantages.
The professor elaborates on this by giving an example.
He mentioned he once saw an advertisement on television. The advertisement is about pots and pans, at first, an expert said their pots and pans are much more expensive than other pots and pans. But since they would provide a life-long term replacement programme, in the long run, actually, customers will save money instead of wasting money.
