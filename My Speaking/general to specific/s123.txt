The reading is about subliminal perception. It states that something that is under our sensory threshold that we cannot consciously be aware of actually can affect our consciousness.
The professor mentioned some researchers let some people watch television and showed them a picture that was about a boy holding a birthcake for less than one second. One group of people saw the boy having a happy face, the other group of people saw the boy having an angry face. And then, the researcher showed them an emotionless boy picture. And the researcher let the two groups of people evaluate the boy's personality. And the group which saw the happy face of the boy deems the boy personality positively. And the other group considers the boy characteristic negatively.
