The reading is about reactance. It states that reactance is when people are restricted from doing something, they will do it even more, whether they are adults or children.
The professor elaborates on this by providing two examples.
First, he said when you are still a boy, you really like to play at a certain playground, and someday, your parents restricted you from going there anymore for no reason. You would most likely to sneak there and play at there even more.
Second, a government prohibits a certain soap from selling in markets, because it is detrimental to the environment. As a result, people will purchase the soap even more than before.
