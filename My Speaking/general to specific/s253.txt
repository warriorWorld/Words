The reading is about cultural lag, it states that when a new technology first appears, people tend to not use it, and gradually by gradually, people will adapt to it.
The professor elaborates on this by giving an example.
He mentioned the telephone which was invented in the 1900s. At first, only businessmen use it, since they need to be productive. Most people think using a telephone is rude, they'd prefer to meet someone in person. And after 30 years, people finally adapted to telephones, they nolonger consider using a telephone is rude except if you call someone at midnight.
