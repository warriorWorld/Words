The reading is about emotional intelligence, it states that when people have high emotional intelligence they can realize their own emotions and know what caused them, and this kind of person could adapt to societies properly.
The professor elaborates on this by giving an example.
He mentioned his daughter once invited her friend to his home, and they needed to decide which movie to watch. They had an argument because of this. But soon, his daughter realized why she was upset, she realized that it was because she was nervous about her new job which required her to take care of a lot of children, and she wanted those children to like her. Then she apologized to her friend.
