The reading is about franchising. It states that if an entrepreneur wants to reduce risk on his investment, he could open a store by franchising. But, in this way he has to obey the headquarters of the company.
The professor said if you want to open a pizza store, you could buy the pizza town right. In this way you don't need to worry about the sale of your pizza anymore, since everyone knows pizza town, they will buy your pizza. And the headquarters of the pizza town will train you. But as a result, you have to decorate your store according to pizza town's regulations. And you don't have any choices on your menu.
