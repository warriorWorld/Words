The reading is about outsider art. It states that some artists would live outside societies without being affected by traditional art, As a result, their art is unique.
The professor mentioned  Darger as an example of this, Darger was born in Chicago, his father had seven children. Darger draws his paintings on his own and has his own unique styles. He had difficulties drawing figures, so he cut out pictures from newspapers and magazines, and pastes them on his own paintings, this makes his paintings look weird. And his paintings are usually longer than other artists' paintings, his paintings usually are nine feet long.
