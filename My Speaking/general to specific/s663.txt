The reading is about the commitment device. It states that when people want to achieve some goals, they will always be interrupted by some immediate desires. And the commitment device can help people conquer this problem.
The professor mentioned himself as an example of this.
He once want to join a ten kilometer running match. So, he need to exercise, but because he has to work, he can only exercise during morning. He setted an alarm, but in the next morning, he didn't manage to wakeup at time. So he ask his friend who also will take the match exercise with him. And because he has the responsibility to his friend, now he can wakeup every morning.
