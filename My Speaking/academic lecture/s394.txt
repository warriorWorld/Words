The lecturer explains how animals move around mountain tops by giving two examples.
Mountain tops are the places that above tree lines, in other words, no trees on mountain tops. And mountain tops are steep, and have a lot of rock and snow. It is very slippery.
Mountain goats use their specially built muscles to conquer this problem, they can easily climb up and down mountain tops.
The bighorn sheep also live on mountain tops, they use their specifically built body features to help them live on mountain tops.
