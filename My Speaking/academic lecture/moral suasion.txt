The lecturer explains moral suasion by giving two examples. She said that moral suasion is a method that utilizes people's morals to prevent them from hurting the environment.
First, she describes a bear called Smoky the Bear, the bear is an adorable character, and the bear said: "Don't hurt my animal friends" and it works well. Because it let people consciously aware to protect the environment.
Secondly, she mentions the recycling movement which let people recycle trash. And when people see their neighbor doing it, they do it too.
These examples demonstrate how moral suasion works.
