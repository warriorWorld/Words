The lecturer explains two kinds of motivations that we humans have, extrinsic and intrinsic motivations.
A child does a lot of small jobs or chores in order to get a small amount of money from his parents, and this is a kind of extrinsic motivation.
Usually, intrinsic motivations can last longer compared to extrinsic motivations. The professor mentioned herself as an example of this, she mentioned that she loves to do exercises in gyms, because exercises can bring her good health, she already did it for five years.
