The lecturer explains the diffusion by giving two examples. Difusion is about cultures mixed together through military invasions, tours and TV shows.
Use newspapers as an example, the words in the newspaper are from a lot of foreign countries, and the print technique is from German, the paper is from China.
Another example is acupuncture. Acupuncuture is from China, in America, a lot of people would use it as a cure for some diseases, even though they don't understand the philosophy behind the acupuncture but they acknowledge the effectiveness of the acupuncture.
