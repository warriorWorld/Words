The lecturer explains food spoiling, she mentioned that food spoil is caused by bacteria proliferation. And there are two factors that affect bacteria proliferation, one is temperature and another is moisture. She says that when you put foods into a cold area they will last much longer than when you put them into a hot area, this indicates how temperature effect food spoiling. Moreover, she mentions that powder milk can persist much longer than liquid milk, this indicates how moisture effect food spoiling.
In conclusion, you could put foods in a refrigerator or make them dry if you want to preserve them longer.
