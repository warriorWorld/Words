The reading is about system thinking. It states that system thinking solves problems in long term by considering the big-picture in a company. The professor elaborates on this by providing an example.
He mentions that there is a company who have an absent problem, and the company hired an expert to solve this issue. The expert found that those employees are not absent because of some obvious reasons such as lack of motivation or just laziness. And the real cause is their health problem, which is caused by their eating habit and lacking exercise. The expert suggests the company build a gym and provides nourishing food at their eatery. One year later, the company's absence problem improved, and continue improving.
